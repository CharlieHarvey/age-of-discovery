About

Age of Discovery is a hard rock band based in the United Kingdom. Formed in 2022, the band consists of Charlotte Cox - Vocals, Charlie Harvey - Vocals and Rhythm Guitar, Sam Hall - Lead Guitar, George Hawkes - Drums, and Samara Tory - Bass.
